Bearded dragon

If a bearded dragon loses (or has lost) its tail it will not grow back. However, with other types of lizards, if their tail falls off it can grow back.

  • Scientific Name:Pogona Vitticeps
  • Average Length: 45-60 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8-10 years
  • Habitat: Desert or large terrarium with heat lamps

Bearded dragons are native to Central Australia, where they are found in semi-arid woodlands and rocky deserts. They are skilled climbers and spend the morning and evening basking on an exposed rock or branch
